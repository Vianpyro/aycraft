Aycraft general rules and regulations

Basic principles

  • 1.Players must respect other players and staff. Any form of insult, discrimination, prejudice to the image of others is punished. Being courteous and polite is essential.

  • 2.The staff is present in the game in order to maintain order and peace on the server, therefore, any form of flood, disrespect or other negative behavior towards a staff member may be punished.

  • 3.Spamming, preventing the correct readability of the chat is prohibited. Avoid unnecessary messages and give maximum priority to private messages.

  • 4.The use of SMS language in game or on the Discord is not recommended. Express yourself in as correct a language as possible.

  • 5.Advertising for any server, religion, product, service, organization or political movement is prohibited. Exceptions may be granted upon request to the Staff.

  • 6.Anyone posting ingame or Discord messages that incite hatred of the server or bring bad mood will be punished. Aycraft is an entertainment space, any conflicts that may exist between a player and the Aycraft Staff must be resolved privately.

  • 7.Aycraft is not a dating site. DO NOT give personal information to another player (email, name, address, phone number...). Beware of disgraceful people. Never agree to meet someone you don't know in the real world.

  • 8.Each player is identified by his Minecraft account and/or his Discord account. Thus, if someone other than you breaches these rules through your Minecraft or Discord account, the entire account will be sanctioned even if you can prove that it was not you (as a natural person) who was using the account at the time. It is therefore strongly discouraged to lend your account/disclose your password. Nevertheless, the staff reserves the right to sanction accounts sharing the same physical owner if the team deems it necessary.

  • 9.It is the Staff (consisting of the administrator, team members and moderators) who make all decisions on the server (sanctions against a player or other). The decisions taken by the Staff are not contestable.

  • 10.A banned player has all his belongings confiscated, including Aycoins or other purchases.

Behaviour in play

  • 1.Any cheating, of any type whatever it is, will be sanctioned by a permanent ban. No mod not included in the whitelist is allowed. It is possible to request the staff to add a mod to this list. Any exploitation of bugs ("usebug") is prohibited. If you face this, contact a moderator. Any abuse will be punished.

  • 2.Any ranked person may have it removed in the event of suspected cheating, pure cheating, inappropriate conduct, judged only and only by the staff.

  • 3.The Aycraft Discord is open to all, therefore, you will be asked for proper attitude.


  • 4.These rules apply to both the game server and the Discord.

Problems, complaints, sanctions

  • 1.Moderators are there to manage the conflicts at stake. A disrespectful player insults you? Call a moderator or any other member of the staff, we will be happy to help you.

  • 2.If no Staff member is connected when you need someone (or if the Staff member in question is not able to solve your problem immediately), you can post a complaint in the dedicated section of the Discord.

  • 3.We believe that dishonest players can change, so we always give our banned players a second chance. If you have just been banned, you can request a debannissement in the Discord lounge provided for this purpose. It will then be decided whether or not to unban you.

Confidentiality

  • 1.It is recalled that in accordance with the Data Protection Act, the User has the right to consult, modify and rectify any personal data brought to Aycraft's attention when using the services offered by the site. This right may be exercised by sending an email to the site owner.

Services
